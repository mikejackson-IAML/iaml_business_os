Frequently Asked Questions

Am I eligible for free alumni access?
If you've attended any IAML certificate program, you're eligible for complimentary access to Quarterly Employment Law Updates for 12 months following your program. This benefit is part of your program investment and reflects our commitment to ongoing support.
What if I miss a live session?
Recordings are available within 24 hours of each session. You can watch at your convenience while still earning CE credits.
Do I earn continuing education credits?
Yes. Each 90-minute session provides 1.5 SHRM PDCs, 1.5 HRCI recertification credits, and 1.5 CLE credits (where applicable).
Can I share my access with colleagues?
Registration is individual. However, your colleagues can register for $397/session, or if they attend an IAML certificate program, they'll receive complimentary access as alumni.
What topics are covered in each session?
Topics vary by quarter based on the most significant employment law developments. Recent sessions have covered NLRB's Cemex and Stericycle decisions, state law updates from California and New York, and emerging AI hiring regulations. We focus on what you need to know now.
How long is each session?
Each live session runs 90 minutes, including Q&A with our faculty. This format provides enough depth to be actionable without consuming your entire day.
Is this the same content as your certificate programs?
No. Quarterly updates focus exclusively on recent developments and emerging trends. They complement the foundational expertise you gain from certificate programs by keeping you current as the law evolves.
When are the sessions held?
Sessions are typically held mid-quarter (January, April, July, October) to provide timely coverage of developments from the previous quarter. Exact dates are announced 4-6 weeks in advance.
