If you are choosing for yourself, start with the finder. If you are choosing for others, start with team training.

I’m not sure which program fits. Where should I start?

Use the Program Finder for a quick recommendation. If you already know the workplace issue, browse by need. If you are planning for more than one person, start with Team Training.

Can I send more than one person?

Yes. IAML can help with individual seats, multiple participants, reserved group seats, and team training options.

Are programs available for individual seats, virtual delivery, or private training?

Some programs may be available as private training delivered virtually or at your location, depending on the audience, timing, and training need.

Who are IAML programs designed for?

IAML programs support HR, employee relations, benefits, legal, compliance, workplace investigation, managers and supervisors, and other management professionals.

Are programs attorney-led?

Yes. IAML programs are led by practicing attorneys and focus on practical workplace law judgment, not abstract legal theory.

Can participants receive professional credit?

SHRM, HRCI, CLE, and CE pathways are available for qualifying programs.
