## 5. Content Restructuring Recommendations

1. **about-us.html**: Add a visible FAQ accordion section using Q&A pairs from the President's
   letter and "Why IAML" content. Schema alone is not enough — visible Q&A gives AI engines
   extractable text even without structured data parsing.

2. **faculty.html**: Add a "Questions About Our Faculty" section (4-5 visible Q&A pairs).
   Content already exists on the page — restructure into explicit Q&A format.

3. **testimonials.html**: Consider adding numeric star ratings to Airtable testimonials.
   This enables a genuine average ratingValue in AggregateRating schema.

4. **All program pages**: Consider adding a brief (2-3 sentence) "In summary" callout box
   at the top of each program page — these quotable summaries are the most AI-extractable
   content format.
