Popular Corporate Training Programs

Here are eight topics organizations request most often. Need something else? We can customize training on any employment law or HR topic your team faces.

Workplace Harassment Prevention

Half-Day to Full-Day

It's not just about checking a legal box. We'll give your employees practical, real-world guidance on recognizing, preventing, and responding to harassment that actually changes behavior and protects your organization from liability.

Who Should Attend
All employees and managers, with separate tracks for leadership and general staff

Employment Law Essentials for Managers

1 Day

Your managers are making employment law decisions every day, whether they know it or not. Every interview question, performance review, and accommodation request carries legal risk. We'll teach them what they need to know to protect your organization.

Who Should Attend
Managers, supervisors, and HR professionals who interact with employees

Conducting Lawful Workplace Investigations

1-2 Days

A botched investigation can turn a manageable complaint into a million-dollar lawsuit. We'll teach your team the exact process for conducting thorough, impartial, legally defensible investigations, from initial complaint through final documentation.

Who Should Attend
HR professionals, managers, investigators, and anyone responsible for workplace culture

FMLA, ADA, and Leave Management

1-2 Days

Navigate the maze of overlapping federal and state leave laws. We'll teach your team how to handle leave requests, reasonable accommodations, and the intersection of FMLA, ADA, workers' comp, and state-specific requirements without triggering costly violations.

Who Should Attend
HR professionals, benefits administrators, and managers who approve leave requests

Performance Management & Progressive Discipline

1 Day

Poor documentation is the #1 reason employers lose wrongful termination cases. We'll show your managers exactly how to document performance issues, deliver difficult feedback, and implement progressive discipline that protects your organization.

Who Should Attend
Managers and HR professionals responsible for performance management and discipline

Wage & Hour Compliance

1 Day

One misclassified exempt employee can cost years of back overtime for your entire department. We'll teach your team FLSA exemption rules, overtime calculations, timekeeping requirements, and how to avoid the costly mistakes that trigger DOL audits and class action lawsuits.

Who Should Attend
HR professionals, payroll teams, and managers who classify positions and approve overtime

Positive Employee Relations

1-2 Days

The best way to avoid employment law problems? Build relationships where employees feel heard before they feel the need to lawyer up. We'll teach your managers practical skills that improve engagement, reduce turnover, and prevent union organizing.

Who Should Attend
Managers, supervisors, and HR professionals focused on culture and retention

Preventing Workplace Violence & Threat Assessment

Half-Day to Full-Day

Most workplace violence is predictable and preventable if your team knows the warning signs. We'll teach you how to identify threats early, conduct effective threat assessments, and implement prevention strategies before tragedy strikes.

Who Should Attend
HR professionals, security teams, managers, and anyone responsible for workplace safety
