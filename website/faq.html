About IAML

IAML (Institute for Applied Management & Law) has been the trusted source for HR and employment law training since 1979. We've trained over 80,000 HR professionals, in-house counsel, and managers through our certificate programs, workshops, and corporate training. Our programs cover everything from employee relations law and workplace investigations to strategic HR leadership and employee benefits.
Our instructors are practicing attorneys and experienced HR professionals who handle employment matters every day—not academics or corporate trainers reading from textbooks. They bring real courtroom experience, actual case examples, and practical strategies you can implement immediately. When you ask a question, you get an answer from someone who's dealt with that exact situation.
Our faculty consists of practicing employment attorneys and senior HR professionals who handle employment law cases and workplace issues daily. They share strategies from actual courtrooms and workplaces, not textbooks. They answer questions from direct experience, not theory.

Continuing Education

Yes. We're accredited for CLE, SHRM, and HRCI credits. Our programs qualify because they're taught by practicing attorneys and HR professionals—it doesn't matter if you attend in-person, virtually, or through corporate training. We handle the paperwork automatically.
IAML programs qualify for SHRM Professional Development Credits (PDCs), HRCI Recertification Credits, and CLE Credits for attorneys. The specific credit hours vary by program—check individual program pages for exact credit amounts.
IAML is an approved provider. We provide a completion certificate with our provider ID. For HRCI, you submit directly to them. For SHRM and CLE, we walk you through the process. It's straightforward—we've done this thousands of times.
