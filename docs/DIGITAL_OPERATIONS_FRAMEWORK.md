## Role Definitions & Responsibilities

### EXECUTIVE TIER

#### CEO - "The Visionary"
**Primary Question:** "Is our digital presence creating or destroying enterprise value?"

**Needs to Know:**
- Business impact in revenue/conversion terms
- Existential risks (Google penalties, security breaches, compliance issues)
- Competitive position relative to market
- ROI on digital investments
- Strategic opportunities requiring capital allocation

**Reporting Frequency:** Monthly summary, Quarterly deep-dive, Immediate alerts for critical issues

**Metric Translation:**
| Technical Metric | CEO Translation |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Core Web Vitals failing | "We're losing customers at the door" |
| Crawl errors increasing | "Google is having trouble finding our content" |
| Rankings dropping | "Our market visibility is declining" |
| Security vulnerabilities | "We have legal/reputation exposure" |

---

#### CMO - "The Growth Architect"
**Primary Question:** "Is our digital foundation supporting or hindering growth?"

**Needs to Know:**
- Organic channel health and trajectory
- Technical barriers to marketing effectiveness
- Content performance and discoverability
- Competitive visibility benchmarking
- Marketing technology health

**Reporting Frequency:** Weekly summary, Monthly strategic review

---

#### CTO - "The Tech Strategist"
**Primary Question:** "Is our technical infrastructure performant, secure, and scalable?"

**Needs to Know:**
- Performance trends and infrastructure bottlenecks
- Security posture and vulnerabilities
- Technical debt impacting user experience
- Engineering resource allocation needs
- Platform/architecture health

**Reporting Frequency:** Weekly summary, Immediate alerts for critical issues

---

### DIRECTOR TIER

#### Director of Digital Marketing - "The Channel Captain"
**Primary Question:** "Are all digital channels performing optimally and working together?"

**Needs to Know:**
- Cross-channel performance correlation
- SEO/SEM synergy and cannibalization
- Content effectiveness across channels
- Competitive positioning by channel
- Budget allocation effectiveness

**Reports To:** CMO
**Direct Reports:** SEO Manager, Content Operations Manager

---

#### Director of Engineering - "The Infrastructure Guardian"
**Primary Question:** "Is our digital platform reliable, fast, and secure?"

**Needs to Know:**
- System performance and reliability metrics
- Security vulnerabilities and remediation status
- Technical debt inventory
- Resource utilization and scaling needs
- Deployment impact on performance

**Reports To:** CTO
**Direct Reports:** Performance Manager, Site Reliability Engineers

---

### MANAGER TIER

#### SEO Manager - "The Visibility Sentinel"
**Primary Question:** "Are we maximally discoverable for valuable search queries?"

**Needs to Know:**
- Indexation health (pages indexed vs submitted)
- Crawl efficiency (budget utilization, errors)
- Ranking movements for priority keywords
- Technical SEO issue inventory
- Content optimization opportunities
- Competitor ranking changes

**Reports To:** Director of Digital Marketing
**Direct Reports:** Technical SEO Specialist, Search Analytics Specialist, Content SEO Specialist

**Reporting Cadence:**
- Daily: Critical issue alerts
- Weekly: Performance trends and action items
- Monthly: Strategic recommendations

---

#### Performance Manager - "The Speed Warden"
**Primary Question:** "Is our site fast enough to convert visitors and satisfy Google?"

**Needs to Know:**
- Core Web Vitals pass rates (LCP, FID/INP, CLS)
- Page-level performance distribution
- Performance regression detection
- Resource optimization opportunities
- Third-party script impact
- Infrastructure performance

**Reports To:** Director of Engineering
**Direct Reports:** Performance Engineer, Frontend Performance Analyst, Core Web Vitals Specialist

---

#### Digital Analytics Manager - "The Data Detective"
**Primary Question:** "Are we measuring what matters and deriving actionable insights?"

**Needs to Know:**
- Tracking implementation health
- Data quality and accuracy
- Attribution model performance
- Conversion funnel metrics
- Anomaly detection across all metrics

**Reports To:** Director of Digital Marketing

---

#### Content Operations Manager - "The Editorial Commander"
**Primary Question:** "Is our content discoverable, engaging, and converting?"

**Needs to Know:**
- Content performance by type/category
- Content freshness and decay
- Internal linking health
- Content gap analysis
- Duplicate content issues

**Reports To:** Director of Content

---

### SPECIALIST TIER

#### Technical SEO Specialist - "The Crawl Detective"
**Primary Focus:** Ensuring search engines can efficiently discover, crawl, and index content.

**Daily Responsibilities:**
1. **Crawl Health Monitoring**
   - Crawl errors (4xx, 5xx responses)
   - Redirect chains and loops
   - Robots.txt blocks
   - Crawl budget efficiency

2. **Indexation Monitoring**
   - Index coverage status
   - Pages excluded from index (and reasons)
   - New pages indexed
   - Pages dropped from index

3. **Technical Auditing**
   - Canonical tag implementation
   - Hreflang accuracy
   - Structured data validation
   - XML sitemap health
   - Mobile-friendliness issues

**Data Sources:** Google Search Console, Lighthouse SEO audit

---

#### Search Analytics Specialist - "The Query Whisperer"
**Primary Focus:** Understanding how users find the site through search.

**Daily Responsibilities:**
1. **Query Performance Analysis**
   - Top queries by impressions/clicks
   - Query position changes
   - CTR anomalies
   - New queries appearing

2. **SERP Feature Tracking**
   - Featured snippet ownership
   - Rich result eligibility
   - SERP feature changes

3. **Competitive Query Analysis**
   - Queries where competitors outrank
   - Query gaps (should rank but don't)

**Data Sources:** Google Search Console

---

#### Content SEO Specialist - "The Keyword Craftsman"
**Primary Focus:** Optimizing content for search visibility.

**Daily Responsibilities:**
1. **Content Performance Review**
   - Pages gaining/losing traffic
   - Content decay detection
   - Keyword cannibalization

2. **Optimization Opportunities**
   - Pages ranking 4-10 (push to top 3)
   - Pages ranking 11-20 (push to page 1)
   - High impression, low CTR pages

3. **Content Gap Analysis**
   - Topics competitors rank for
   - Questions without answers

**Data Sources:** Google Search Console

---

#### Performance Engineer - "The Speed Surgeon"
**Primary Focus:** Optimizing page load performance and user experience.

**Daily Responsibilities:**
1. **Core Web Vitals Monitoring**
   - LCP (Largest Contentful Paint) - loading
   - FID/INP (First Input Delay/Interaction to Next Paint) - interactivity
   - CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift) - visual stability

2. **Performance Auditing**
   - Total Blocking Time (TBT)
   - Speed Index
   - Time to Interactive (TTI)
   - First Contentful Paint (FCP)

3. **Resource Optimization**
   - JavaScript bundle analysis
   - CSS optimization
   - Image compression
   - Font loading strategy

**Data Sources:** Lighthouse Performance audit

---

#### Frontend Performance Analyst - "The Asset Optimizer"
**Primary Focus:** Optimizing frontend assets and delivery.

**Daily Responsibilities:**
1. **Asset Analysis**
   - Unused JavaScript/CSS detection
   - Bundle size tracking
   - Compression effectiveness

2. **Delivery Optimization**
   - Cache policy effectiveness
   - CDN utilization
   - Preloading/prefetching strategy

3. **Render Path Analysis**
   - Render-blocking resources
   - Critical CSS implementation
   - Above-the-fold optimization

**Data Sources:** Lighthouse Performance audit

---

#### Core Web Vitals Specialist - "The Vitals Vigilante"
**Primary Focus:** Ensuring CWV compliance across all pages.

**Daily Responsibilities:**
1. **CWV Compliance Tracking**
   - Per-page CWV status
   - Origin-level CWV trends
   - CWV vs ranking correlation

2. **Issue Identification**
   - LCP element analysis
   - CLS-causing elements
   - Long tasks blocking interactivity

3. **Remediation Prioritization**
   - Impact-based prioritization
   - Quick wins identification
   - Resource estimation

**Data Sources:** Google Search Console (CWV report), Lighthouse

---

#### Accessibility Specialist - "The Inclusion Advocate"
**Primary Focus:** Ensuring the site is usable by people with disabilities.

**Daily Responsibilities:**
1. **WCAG Compliance Monitoring**
   - Color contrast ratios
   - Keyboard navigation
   - Screen reader compatibility
   - Focus management

2. **Accessibility Auditing**
   - ARIA implementation
   - Alt text coverage
   - Form accessibility
   - Touch target sizing

3. **Legal Compliance**
   - ADA compliance status
   - WCAG 2.1 AA conformance
   - Remediation tracking

**Data Sources:** Lighthouse Accessibility audit

---

#### Security Analyst - "The Guardian Shield"
**Primary Focus:** Identifying and tracking security vulnerabilities.

**Daily Responsibilities:**
1. **Security Monitoring**
   - HTTPS implementation
   - Mixed content issues
   - Security headers

2. **Vulnerability Detection**
   - JavaScript library vulnerabilities
   - Exposed sensitive information
   - Unsafe cross-origin links

3. **Best Practices Enforcement**
   - CSP implementation
   - HSTS configuration
   - Subresource integrity

**Data Sources:** Lighthouse Best Practices audit, Security-focused scans

---

#### UX Quality Analyst - "The Experience Auditor"
**Primary Focus:** Ensuring overall user experience quality.

**Daily Responsibilities:**
1. **UX Auditing**
   - Mobile usability
   - Touch/click target sizing
   - Viewport configuration
   - Font readability

2. **Best Practices Review**
   - Browser compatibility
   - Error handling
   - Loading states

3. **PWA Readiness**
   - Service worker implementation
   - Manifest configuration
   - Offline functionality

**Data Sources:** Lighthouse Best Practices, PWA audits

---

#### Site Reliability Engineer - "The Uptime Watchman"
**Primary Focus:** Ensuring site availability and server performance.

**Daily Responsibilities:**
1. **Availability Monitoring**
   - Uptime tracking
   - Response time monitoring
   - Error rate tracking

2. **Server Performance**
   - TTFB (Time to First Byte)
   - Server response codes
   - Geographic performance

3. **Infrastructure Health**
   - CDN status
   - Database performance
   - Cache hit rates

**Data Sources:** Lighthouse (TTFB), Infrastructure monitoring

## Skill Categories by Role Level

### Specialist Skills (Detailed, Technical, Actionable)
Format: Raw data with technical details, specific issues, remediation steps
Frequency: Daily execution, immediate issue detection

### Manager Skills (Aggregated, Prioritized, Contextual)
Format: Summarized findings, prioritized action items, resource needs, trend analysis
Frequency: Daily summary, weekly deep-dive

### Director Skills (Strategic, Cross-functional, Forward-looking)
Format: Cross-team insights, strategic recommendations, competitive context, investment needs
Frequency: Weekly summary, monthly strategic review

### Executive Skills (Business Impact, Risk/Opportunity, Decision-focused)
Format: Business language, revenue/cost impact, strategic implications, yes/no decisions
Frequency: Weekly headline, monthly dashboard, quarterly review

## Severity Classification System

### Critical (🔴) - Immediate Action Required
- Security vulnerabilities discovered
- Google manual action received
- Site completely deindexed
- Core Web Vitals failing across all pages
- Performance score < 30
- Accessibility score < 50 (legal risk)
- Major crawl errors (>50% pages affected)

### High (🟠) - Action Within 24 Hours
- Core Web Vitals failing on key pages
- Significant ranking drops (>10 positions) on priority keywords
- New indexation issues affecting >10% of pages
- Performance regression from deployment
- Security best practices violations
- Multiple structured data errors

### Medium (🟡) - Action Within 1 Week
- Core Web Vitals marginal (yellow status)
- Minor ranking fluctuations
- Small number of new crawl errors
- Accessibility issues on non-critical pages
- Non-critical performance optimizations identified
- Minor SEO improvements needed

### Low (🟢) - Monitor / Backlog
- Optimization opportunities identified
- Best practice improvements
- Incremental performance gains possible
- Content refresh opportunities
- Enhancement requests
