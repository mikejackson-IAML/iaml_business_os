# Personal Journal

> A space for reflection, processing, and capturing insights.

---

## Structure

```
journal/
├── README.md           # This file
├── daily/              # Daily entries (YYYY-MM-DD.md)
├── weekly/             # Weekly reviews (YYYY-WXX.md)
├── monthly/            # Monthly reflections (YYYY-MM.md)
└── insights/           # Standalone insights and realizations
```

---

## Purpose

This journal serves multiple purposes:
1. **Processing** - Work through challenges and decisions
2. **Pattern Recognition** - Spot trends in energy, productivity, mood
3. **Accountability** - Track commitments to yourself
4. **Gratitude** - Notice what's working
5. **Learning Capture** - Document lessons so they stick

## Journal Prompts

When you're stuck, try these:

### For Processing
- What's weighing on me right now?
- What am I avoiding and why?
- What would I do if I wasn't afraid?

### For Clarity
- What's the real problem here?
- What do I actually want?
- What would success look like?

### For Growth
- What's this situation teaching me?
- How is this making me stronger?
- What would future me thank me for?

### For Gratitude
- What went right today?
- Who helped me recently?
- What am I taking for granted?
