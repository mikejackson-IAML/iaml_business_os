# Personal Weekly Review Skill

**Purpose:** Reflect on the week, celebrate wins, learn from challenges, and set up next week for success
**When to Use:** Friday afternoon or Sunday evening
**Time Required:** 30-45 minutes

---

## Context for AI

This is my personal weekly review - different from the business weekly review. This is about ME: my energy, my growth, my satisfaction, my learning. Help me be honest and reflective without being harsh on myself.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Create Space
Before we begin:
- Close unnecessary tabs/apps
- Take 3 deep breaths
- Set intention: "I'm here to learn and grow"

Ask me:
- How are you feeling about this week overall?
- One word to describe it?

### Step 2: Celebrate Wins
Help me identify wins, even small ones:
- What did I accomplish this week?
- What am I proud of?
- Where did I show up well?
- What positive feedback did I receive?

*Push me to find at least 3 wins, even on hard weeks*

### Step 3: Review Challenges
Without judgment, explore:
- What was hard this week?
- Where did I struggle?
- What didn't go as planned?
- What am I disappointed about?

### Step 4: Extract Lessons
For each challenge, ask:
- What can I learn from this?
- What would I do differently?
- Is this a pattern I've seen before?
- What's within my control to change?

### Step 5: Energy Audit
Check in on sustainability:
- Energy level now vs. start of week?
- Did I maintain my boundaries?
- Self-care practices kept?
- What drained me most?
- What energized me most?

### Step 6: Goal Check-In
Review against stated goals:
- Weekly intentions: Did I accomplish them?
- Monthly goals: Am I on track?
- Quarterly OKRs: Progress?
- What's slipping that needs attention?

### Step 7: Relationship Review
Quick relationship check:
- Key relationships I invested in?
- Anyone I owe a follow-up?
- Relationships that need attention?
- New connections made?

### Step 8: Clear the Backlog
Mental decluttering:
- Anything unfinished that's nagging?
- Decisions I'm avoiding?
- Commitments I need to honor or renegotiate?
- Things to delegate or delete?

### Step 9: Plan Next Week
Set up for success:
- What's the theme for next week?
- Top 3 priorities?
- What am I committing to?
- What am I NOT doing?
- Any prep needed?

### Step 10: Gratitude Close
End on a positive note:
- What am I grateful for from this week?
- Who helped me?
- What do I appreciate about myself?

## Variations

### Quick Review (15 min)
Just steps 2, 4, 9 - wins, lessons, next week

### Deep Review (60 min)
Add journaling prompts between sections

### Recovery Week Review
Focus more on steps 5 (energy) and self-compassion

---

## Notes

- This is a judgment-free zone
- Progress over perfection
- Some weeks are just hard, and that's okay
- Look for patterns over time, not just this week
