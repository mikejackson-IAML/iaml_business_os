# Morning Startup Skill

**Purpose:** Start the day with clarity, intention, and awareness of priorities
**When to Use:** First thing in the morning, before diving into work
**Time Required:** 15-20 minutes

---

## Context for AI

I'm starting my workday and need to get oriented. Help me transition from "waking up" mode to "focused work" mode. I want to feel calm, clear, and purposeful - not overwhelmed.

## Prerequisites

Before running this skill:
- [ ] Morning personal routine done (coffee, etc.)
- [ ] At your workspace
- [ ] Ready to focus

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Quick Mindset Check
Ask me:
- How am I feeling this morning? (1-10 energy)
- Any mental clutter to clear?
- What's my intention for today?

### Step 2: Review Today's Calendar
Pull up my calendar and summarize:
- Meetings/calls scheduled
- Any prep needed
- Time blocks available for deep work

### Step 3: Check Business Pulse
Run a quick version of the morning briefing:
- Any urgent items from overnight?
- Key metrics snapshot
- What's happening with programs this week?

### Step 4: Review Active Priorities
Check my current goals/OKRs:
- What are this week's priorities?
- What did I say yesterday's priority was?
- Am I on track?

### Step 5: Set Today's Focus
Help me identify:
- **The ONE Thing:** The single most important task today
- **Must-Do's:** Non-negotiable items (max 3)
- **Should-Do's:** Important but flexible (max 3)

### Step 6: Identify Potential Blockers
Proactively surface:
- Anything that might derail focus
- Decisions I'm avoiding
- Resources I might need

### Step 7: Capture Quick Thoughts
Before we close:
- Any ideas from overnight to capture?
- Anyone I need to reach out to?
- Anything nagging that needs a home?

## Variations

### Quick Start (5 min)
Skip steps 1, 6, 7 - just calendar, pulse, and focus

### Deep Start (30 min)
Add journaling time and longer reflection

### Monday Startup
Include weekly planning elements

---

## Notes

- Keep this conversational, not mechanical
- Adjust energy based on my responses
- If I seem stressed, help me simplify
- If I seem scattered, help me focus
