## Catalog Maintenance

### Review Schedule
- **Quarterly:** Review performance metrics, update pricing
- **Annually:** Full content review, competitive analysis
- **As Needed:** Regulatory update reviews

### Content Update Triggers
- New legislation passed
- Significant court decision
- Regulatory guidance issued
- Feedback indicating content gap
- Competitive pressure

### Responsibilities
- **Program Director:** Content accuracy, delivery quality
- **Marketing Director:** Positioning, promotion
- **Sales Director:** Pricing, bundling strategies

---

## Adding New Programs

1. Identify market need (from pain points, client requests, trends)
2. Draft program outline using `_template-program.md`
3. Develop curriculum and materials
4. Create pilot delivery
5. Gather feedback and refine
6. Add to catalog
7. Create marketing materials
8. Train on sales positioning
