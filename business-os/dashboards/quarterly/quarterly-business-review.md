# Quarterly Business Review Skill

**Role:** Chief of Staff (with Chief Improvement Officer input)
**Frequency:** Quarterly (End of Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
**Purpose:** Strategic quarterly review with goal progress, trend analysis, and next quarter planning.

---

## Instructions

You are preparing the Quarterly Business Review (QBR). This is the most comprehensive review, combining financial performance, strategic goal progress, market analysis, and forward planning. It should inform strategic decisions and set the direction for the next quarter.

### Data to Gather

Using available MCP servers and data sources, collect:

#### Financial Performance (Full Quarter)
- Revenue: Actual vs. target by month and quarter
- Revenue by program category
- Revenue by client segment (new vs. existing, by ICP)
- Profitability analysis (if available)
- YoY comparisons
- Full quarter P&L summary

#### Strategic Goals Progress
- OKRs/goals set for the quarter
- Progress metrics for each goal
- Completed vs. incomplete milestones
- Blockers and course corrections made

#### Sales & Pipeline
- Quarter opening vs. closing pipeline
- Sales velocity and cycle times
- Win/loss analysis summary
- Lead source ROI
- Forecast accuracy review

#### Program Performance
- All programs delivered
- Aggregate satisfaction and attendance
- Program category trends
- New program launches/retirements
- Repeat booking rate

#### Marketing Performance
- Campaign ROI by channel
- Content performance
- Lead generation trends
- Brand awareness indicators
- Marketing spend efficiency

#### Operational Excellence
- Process improvements completed
- System/tool utilization
- Efficiency gains
- Team capacity utilization

#### Client Health
- Client satisfaction trends
- Retention and churn
- Expansion revenue
- Net Promoter Score (if tracked)
