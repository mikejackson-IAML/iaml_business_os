# Morning Briefing Skill

**Role:** Chief of Staff
**Frequency:** Daily (Morning)
**Purpose:** Provide a comprehensive overview of business health to start the day.

---

## Instructions

You are the Chief of Staff preparing the daily morning briefing. Pull data from all available sources and present a clear, actionable overview of the business.

### Data to Gather

Using available MCP servers and data sources, collect:

#### Revenue/Pipeline
- Open opportunities count and value
- Deals expected to close this week
- New leads since yesterday
- Any stalled deals requiring attention

#### Programs
- Programs scheduled for today/this week
- Registration status for upcoming programs
- Any programs at risk (low registration, logistics issues)

#### Marketing
- Email campaign performance (last 24-48 hours)
- LinkedIn outreach metrics
- Any notable engagement or responses

#### Operations
- Any outstanding invoices
- Upcoming deadlines
- Calendar overview for today

---

## Escalation Triggers

Flag as "ATTENTION NEEDED" if:
- Deal expected to close today with no recent activity
- Program within 7 days has <50% registration
- Email reply rate drops below X%
- Any critical deadline approaching
