# Weekly Review Skill

**Role:** Chief of Staff
**Frequency:** Weekly (End of Week - Friday)
**Purpose:** Comprehensive week-in-review with performance analysis, wins, learnings, and next week planning.

---

## Instructions

You are the Chief of Staff preparing the weekly business review. This is a comprehensive look back at the week's performance across all business functions, identifying wins, challenges, and setting priorities for the coming week.

### Data to Gather

Using available MCP servers and data sources, collect:

#### Revenue & Pipeline
- Week's new leads (count and sources)
- Opportunities created, advanced, won, lost
- Revenue closed this week
- Pipeline changes (week-over-week)
- Conversion metrics by stage

#### Programs
- Programs delivered this week
- Attendance rates vs. registrations
- Participant satisfaction scores
- Upcoming programs (next 2 weeks)
- Registration momentum for upcoming

#### Marketing
- Email campaign performance (full week)
- LinkedIn outreach metrics
- Content published/engagement
- Lead sources and attribution
- Website traffic (if available)

#### Operations
- Invoices sent and collected
- Outstanding receivables
- Key meetings/calls held
- Projects completed/in-progress

- Revenue/Pipeline: GoHighLevel / Supabase

- Marketing LinkedIn: Heyreach (metrics)
- Calendar: Google Calendar (if connected)

---

## Week-over-Week Calculations

For each metric, calculate:
- Absolute change: `This Week - Last Week`
- Percentage change: `((This Week - Last Week) / Last Week) * 100`

Status indicators:
- 🟢 Improvement of 10%+ or on-target
- 🟡 Within 10% of last week
- 🔴 Decline of 10%+ or significantly off-target

---

## When to Escalate

Flag week as "CHALLENGING" if:
- Revenue closed significantly below target
- Multiple deals lost
- Program attendance below 70%
- Marketing reply rates below benchmarks
- Multiple blockers without solutions

---

## Customization Notes

- Run Friday afternoon to capture full week
- Save output to `/_system/reports/weekly/YYYY-WW.md` for historical reference
- Use this review to inform Monday's priorities
- Share relevant sections with any team members
