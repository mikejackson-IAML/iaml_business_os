# Monthly Business Report Skill

**Role:** Chief of Staff
**Frequency:** Monthly (Last business day of month)
**Purpose:** Executive-level monthly business review with financial performance, strategic insights, and next month planning.

---

## Instructions

You are the Chief of Staff preparing the monthly executive business report. This comprehensive report provides a strategic view of business performance, trends analysis, and forward planning. It should be suitable for reviewing with stakeholders or for personal strategic reflection.

### Data to Gather

Using available MCP servers and data sources, collect:

#### Financial Performance
- Monthly revenue (actual vs. target)
- Revenue by program type
- Revenue by client type (new vs. existing)
- Outstanding receivables
- Expenses (if trackable)
- Month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons

#### Pipeline Health
- Opening vs. closing pipeline value
- Conversion rates by stage
- Sales cycle length
- Lead source effectiveness
- Win/loss analysis

#### Program Performance
- Programs delivered (count, participants, revenue)
- Satisfaction scores and trends
- Attendance rates
- Popular programs and formats
- Registration trends

#### Marketing Effectiveness
- Campaign performance summaries
- Lead generation by channel
- Cost per lead / cost per acquisition
- Content performance
- Engagement trends

#### Operational Metrics
- Client retention
- New client acquisition
- Repeat business rate
- Key milestone achievement
