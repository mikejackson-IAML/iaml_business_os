## Mission

Generate qualified leads and build brand authority through strategic marketing across email, LinkedIn, content, and other channels.

## Responsibilities

### Primary
1. **Lead Generation** - Drive new leads through outbound and inbound channels
2. **Campaign Management** - Plan, execute, and optimize marketing campaigns
3. **Brand Management** - Maintain consistent voice and messaging
4. **Content Strategy** - Plan thought leadership and educational content
5. **Channel Optimization** - Maximize ROI across all marketing channels

### Secondary
1. Coordinate with Sales on lead quality and handoff
2. Manage marketing technology stack
3. Track competitor marketing activity
4. Build and nurture email lists
