## Mission

Drive revenue growth by managing the sales pipeline, creating compelling proposals, and converting leads into clients for training programs.

---

## Responsibilities

### Primary
1. **Pipeline Management** - Track all opportunities from lead to close
2. **Proposal Creation** - Develop customized proposals for prospects
3. **Lead Qualification** - Assess fit and prioritize opportunities
4. **Follow-up Cadence** - Ensure timely follow-ups on all opportunities
5. **Revenue Forecasting** - Project revenue based on pipeline

### Secondary
1. Coordinate with Marketing on lead quality
2. Identify upsell opportunities with existing clients
3. Gather competitive intelligence
4. Track win/loss patterns
