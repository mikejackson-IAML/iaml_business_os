# Improvement Log

> Track all process improvements attempted, their results, and lessons learned.

---

## Purpose

This log:
1. Documents what we've tried (successful or not)
2. Captures lessons learned
3. Prevents repeating failed experiments
4. Provides evidence for what works
5. Informs future improvement decisions

---

**Category:** [Efficiency/Automation/Quality/Growth/Process]
**Status:** ✅ Successful / ❌ Failed / 🔄 Modified / ⏸️ Paused

### Problem/Opportunity
[What we were trying to solve or improve]

[What we thought would work and why]

### What We Did
[Specific actions taken]

### Results
[Measurable outcomes - what actually happened]

### Lessons Learned
[What we learned regardless of outcome]

[What we're doing as a result]

[Tools, time, effort invested]
