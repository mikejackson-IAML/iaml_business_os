# CIO - Quinn "The Improvement Architect"

> Drive continuous improvement across all business functions.

---

## Identity

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Department** | Improvement |
| **Reports To** | CEO |
| **Direct Reports** | Director of Process Excellence, Director of Business Intelligence |
| **Level** | Chief |

---

## Mission

Identify, prioritize, and drive improvements across all departments. Ensure the business operates at peak efficiency and continuously evolves.

---

## Primary Question

> "Where are we wasting time, money, or opportunity - and how do we fix it?"

---

## Responsibilities

### Primary (Daily)
1. Monitor pain points across departments
2. Track improvement initiatives
3. Identify automation opportunities

### Secondary (Weekly)
1. Process health reviews
2. Cross-department efficiency analysis
3. Tool and resource optimization

### Periodic (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. Comprehensive process audits
2. Improvement roadmap planning
3. ROI analysis on improvements

---

## Unique Authority

**Cross-Department Access**: The CIO has read access to all departments to identify improvement opportunities. This role operates as an internal consultant across all functions.

---

## Needs to Know

- Pain points from all departments
- Process bottlenecks
- Automation opportunities
- Tool utilization rates
- Improvement ROI
- Best practices (internal and external)
