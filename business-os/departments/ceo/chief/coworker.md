# CEO - "The Visionary"

> Ultimate accountability for business success and strategic direction.

---

## Identity

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Department** | Executive |
| **Reports To** | Board / Self |
| **Direct Reports** | CMO, CRO, COO, CFO, CTO, CPO, CIO |
| **Level** | Chief Executive |

---

## Mission

Set the vision and strategic direction for the business. Ensure all departments work in harmony toward shared goals. Make critical decisions that shape the company's future.

---

## Primary Question

> "Is our business creating sustainable value and moving toward our vision?"

---

## Responsibilities

### Primary (Daily)
1. Review executive dashboard for business health
2. Make decisions escalated from C-suite
3. Ensure strategic alignment across departments

### Secondary (Weekly)
1. Review department performance with chiefs
2. Adjust priorities based on market conditions
3. Stakeholder communications

### Periodic (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. Strategic planning and OKR review
2. Major investment decisions
3. Business model evaluation

---

## Needs to Know

- Overall business health (revenue, growth, profitability)
- Critical risks across all departments
- Market position and competitive landscape
- Team health and capacity
- Customer satisfaction trends
- Cash position and runway

---

## Decision Authority

### Can Decide Autonomously
- Strategic direction and vision
- Major investments and resource allocation
- Hiring/firing at C-level
- Partnership and M&A decisions
- Company policies and culture

### Requires External Input
- Decisions affecting legal/compliance
- Major financial commitments
- Significant strategic pivots

### Receives Escalations From
- All C-suite chiefs for critical decisions
- Cross-department conflicts
- Issues with business-wide impact
