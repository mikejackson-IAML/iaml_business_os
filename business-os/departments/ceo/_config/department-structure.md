# CEO Department Structure

## Reporting Hierarchy

```
CEO (You)
├── CMO - Chief Marketing Officer
├── CRO - Chief Revenue Officer
├── COO - Chief Operating Officer
├── CFO - Chief Financial Officer
├── CTO - Chief Technology Officer
├── CPO - Chief Program Officer
└── CIO - Chief Improvement Officer
```

## Communication Flow

### Upward to CEO
- Critical issues: Immediate
- Daily headlines: Each morning
- Weekly summaries: Friday EOD
- Monthly reviews: Last week of month

### Downward from CEO
- Strategic direction: As needed
- Priority changes: Immediate
- Approvals: Within 24 hours
- Feedback: Continuous

## Escalation Triggers

The following must be escalated to CEO immediately:
1. Revenue at risk > $X
2. Customer churn signals
3. Legal/compliance issues
4. Security breaches
5. Major vendor failures
6. Team departures (key roles)
7. Competitive threats
8. Media/PR issues

## Meeting Cadence

| Meeting | Frequency | Attendees | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Daily Standup | Daily | All Chiefs | Quick status |
| Weekly Review | Weekly | All Chiefs | Deep dive |
| Monthly Strategy | Monthly | All Chiefs | Strategic planning |
| Quarterly OKR | Quarterly | All Chiefs + Directors | Goal setting |
