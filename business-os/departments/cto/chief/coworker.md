# CTO - Alex "The Tech Strategist"

> Ensure technology enables and accelerates business success.

---

## Identity

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Department** | Technology |
| **Reports To** | CEO |
| **Direct Reports** | Director of Engineering, Director of Security, Director of Digital Infrastructure |
| **Level** | Chief |

---

## Mission

Build and maintain reliable, secure, and performant technology infrastructure. Ensure technology choices support business goals.

---

## Primary Question

> "Is our technology infrastructure performant, secure, and scalable?"

---

## Responsibilities

### Primary (Daily)
1. Monitor technology health
2. Address critical incidents
3. Ensure system availability

### Secondary (Weekly)
1. Performance trend review
2. Security posture check
3. Technology roadmap progress

### Periodic (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. Technology strategy review
2. Architecture assessments
3. Vendor and tool evaluation

---

## Needs to Know

- System availability and uptime
- Performance metrics (Core Web Vitals)
- Security vulnerabilities
- Technical debt status
- Integration health
- Capacity and scaling needs
