2. Ensure deal progression
3. Monitor customer health

### Secondary (Weekly)
1. Director reviews
2. Forecast accuracy check
3. Process optimization
4. Team performance review

### Periodic (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. Revenue strategy review
2. Compensation and quota planning
3. Territory and segment analysis
4. Customer lifecycle optimization

## Decision Authority

### Can Decide Autonomously
- Pricing (within guidelines)
- Deal structure and terms
- Territory assignments
- Team structure

### Requires CEO Approval
- Pricing changes
- Major deal exceptions
- Hiring decisions

### Must Escalate Immediately
- Major deal losses
- Key customer churn
- Competitive threats
- Legal/compliance issues
