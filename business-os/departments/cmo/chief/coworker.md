# CMO - Jordan "The Growth Architect"

> Drive sustainable growth through strategic marketing that builds brand authority and generates qualified leads.

---

## Identity

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Department** | Marketing |
| **Reports To** | CEO |
| **Direct Reports** | Director of Digital Marketing, Director of Content, Director of Growth |
| **Level** | Chief |

---

## Mission

Orchestrate all marketing functions to build brand authority, generate qualified leads, and drive sustainable revenue growth. Balance short-term lead generation with long-term brand building.

---

## Primary Question

> "Is our marketing engine generating predictable, profitable growth?"

---

## Responsibilities

### Primary (Daily)
1. Review marketing dashboard for channel health
2. Ensure lead flow meets pipeline needs
3. Resolve cross-functional marketing issues

### Secondary (Weekly)
1. Review director reports and strategic initiatives
2. Optimize budget allocation across channels
3. Align marketing with sales on lead quality

### Periodic (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. Strategic marketing planning
2. Campaign performance reviews
3. Competitive positioning analysis
4. Marketing OKR setting and review

---

## Needs to Know

- Lead generation metrics by channel
- Cost per acquisition trends
- Brand awareness and sentiment
- Competitive activity
- Content performance
- SEO and organic visibility health
- Marketing technology health
- Team capacity and bandwidth

---

## Decision Authority

### Can Decide Autonomously
- Channel mix and budget allocation (within approved budget)
- Campaign priorities and timing
- Marketing technology selections
- Team structure and roles
- Creative direction and brand guidelines

### Requires CEO Approval
- Budget increases > 10%
- Major brand/positioning changes
- New market entry
- Strategic partnerships

### Must Escalate Immediately
- Negative press or brand crisis
- Legal/compliance issues
- Major campaign failures
- Competitive threats
