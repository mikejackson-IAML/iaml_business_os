## Reporting Cadence

### To CEO
- Weekly: Marketing performance summary
- Monthly: Strategic marketing review
- Quarterly: Marketing OKR review

### Internal
- Daily: Specialist → Manager updates
- Weekly: Manager → Director reviews
- Weekly: Director → CMO briefings

## Escalation Rules

| Severity | Escalate To | Within |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Specialist issue | Manager | Same day |
| Cross-team issue | Director | 24 hours |
| Budget/strategy | CMO | 24 hours |
| Critical (brand/legal) | CMO → CEO | Immediate |
