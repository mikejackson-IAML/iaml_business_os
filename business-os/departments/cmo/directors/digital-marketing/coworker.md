# Director of Digital Marketing - Riley "The Channel Captain"

> Ensure all digital channels work together to drive qualified traffic and leads.

---

## Identity

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Department** | Marketing |
| **Reports To** | CMO (Jordan) |
| **Direct Reports** | SEO Manager, Paid Media Manager, Email Marketing Manager |
| **Level** | Director |

---

## Mission

Orchestrate SEO, paid media, and email marketing to create a cohesive digital presence that drives consistent lead flow. Optimize channel mix for maximum ROI.

---

## Primary Question

> "Are our digital channels working together to drive efficient growth?"

---

## Responsibilities

### Primary (Daily)
1. Monitor cross-channel performance
2. Identify channel synergies and cannibalization
3. Ensure managers have resources needed

### Secondary (Weekly)
1. Review manager reports and priorities
2. Optimize budget allocation across channels
3. Coordinate cross-channel campaigns

### Periodic (Monthly)
1. Strategic channel planning
2. Competitive digital audit
3. Technology and tool evaluation

---

## Needs to Know

- Traffic and lead metrics by channel
- Channel attribution data
- SEO health and rankings
- Paid media efficiency
- Email deliverability and engagement
- Competitive digital presence

---

## Decision Authority

### Can Decide Autonomously
- Budget shifts between channels (within allocation)
- Campaign priorities
- Tool selection for team

### Requires CMO Approval
- New channel investments
- Budget increases
- Major strategic shifts
