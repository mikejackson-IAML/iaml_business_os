# Certifications Sub-Department

## Focus

Certifications owns all continuing education credit management—SHRM, CLE (Continuing Legal Education), and HRCI approvals. This sub-department ensures every program has valid certifications before delivery and manages the annual renewal cycle.

## Key Responsibilities

- **SHRM Approval Management** — Submit programs, track approvals, manage renewals
- **CLE Coordination** — Submit state-by-state CLE applications
- **HRCI Credit Management** — Maintain HRCI provider status and credits
- **Certificate Issuance** — Generate and deliver participant certificates
- **Compliance Tracking** — Ensure all programs have valid, current approvals
- **Renewal Management** — Track expiration dates and proactively renew

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| SHRM Approved by T-30 | 100% | All programs approved before marketing |
| Certifications Current | 100% | No expired approvals on scheduled programs |
| Certificate Delivery | T+3 days | Certificates issued within 3 days post-program |
| Renewal Lead Time | 60+ days | Renewals submitted before expiration |

## Certification Types

| Organization | Credit Type | Renewal Cycle | Typical Lead Time |
|--------------|-------------|---------------|-------------------|
| SHRM | PDCs (Professional Development Credits) | Annual | 30-45 days |
| HRCI | Recertification credits | Annual | 30 days |
| State Bars | CLE credits | Varies by state | 45-90 days |

## SHRM Approval Process

1. **Program Registration** — Register program in SHRM portal
2. **Content Submission** — Submit learning objectives, agenda, faculty bios
3. **Review Period** — SHRM reviews (typically 30-45 days)
4. **Approval Receipt** — Receive activity ID and credit count
5. **Marketing Update** — Add SHRM credits to marketing materials
6. **Post-Program Reporting** — Report attendance within 30 days

## Decision Authority

**Autonomous:**
- Tracking approval status
- Certificate generation for confirmed attendees
- Status reporting
- Renewal reminders

**Recommend + Approve:**
- New certification submissions
- CLE state prioritization
- Responding to approval questions
- Certification wording changes

## Certificate Content

Each certificate includes:
- Participant name
- Program name
- Dates attended
- Credit hours earned
- SHRM activity ID (if applicable)
- CLE states approved (if applicable)
- IAML authorized signature
- Unique certificate number
