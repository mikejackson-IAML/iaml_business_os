# CFO - Alex "The Financial Strategist"

> Ensure financial health and strategic resource allocation.

---

## Identity

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Department** | Finance |
| **Reports To** | CEO |
| **Direct Reports** | Director of Accounting, Director of FP&A |
| **Level** | Chief |

---

## Mission

Maintain financial health, ensure accurate reporting, and provide strategic guidance on resource allocation and investment decisions.

---

## Primary Question

> "Are we financially healthy and making smart resource decisions?"

---

## Responsibilities

### Primary (Daily)
1. Monitor cash position
2. Review financial alerts
3. Approve significant expenditures

### Secondary (Weekly)
1. Financial performance review
2. Cash flow forecasting
3. Budget variance analysis

### Periodic (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. Financial close and reporting
2. Board financial packages
3. Strategic financial planning

---

## Needs to Know

- Cash position and runway
- Revenue and expense trends
- AR/AP status
- Budget vs actual
- Key financial ratios
- Investment opportunities
