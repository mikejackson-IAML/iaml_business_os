# Project Milestones: Lead Intelligence System

## v1.0 Lead Intelligence CRM (Shipped: 2026-01-27)

**Delivered:** Full CRM module replacing the existing leads dashboard with contacts, companies, opportunities, AI search, and external integrations.

**Phases completed:** 1-5 (22 plans total)

**Key accomplishments:**
- Database schema with 11 tables, views, triggers, and indexes deployed to Supabase
- Filterable contact list with 15 filter types, paginated table, and data health metrics
- Contact profiles with 6 tabs (Overview, Attendance, Email, Company, Notes, Enrichment)
- AI-powered natural language search via Claude Haiku with removable filter pills
- AI contact intelligence summaries via Claude Sonnet with caching
- SmartLead campaign integration, Apollo/PhantomBuster enrichment, Find Colleagues workflow
- Bulk actions (add to campaign, enrich, set follow-up) for multiple selected contacts
- Dual-pipeline opportunities system with kanban drag-and-drop and table views
- Opportunity detail pages with stage advancement, contact roles, notes, and file attachments

**Stats:**
- 103 files created
- 12,651 lines of TypeScript/TSX/SQL
- 5 phases, 22 plans
- 1 day build (2026-01-27)
