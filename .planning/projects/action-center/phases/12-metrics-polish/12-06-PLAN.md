## Goal

Show department-level task metrics for department heads / admins.

## Requirements Covered

- MET-03 (department metrics)

## Context

Access control decision: department heads see their department, admins see system-wide. Since v1 is single-user (CEO), this effectively means admin sees all departments.

The `department_task_summary` view already provides:
- open_count, overdue_count, actionable_count
- critical_count, high_priority_count
- created_last_7_days, completed_last_7_days
- completion_rate_30d, avg_completion_days_30d
