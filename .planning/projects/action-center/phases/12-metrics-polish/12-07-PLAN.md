## Goal

Show system-wide task overview for admins.

## Requirements Covered

## Context

The `system_task_summary` view provides system-wide aggregates:
- total_open, total_overdue, total_actionable
- critical_count, high_priority_count
- created_last_7_days, completed_last_7_days
- completion_rate_7d

This is the "big picture" view for understanding overall task health.
