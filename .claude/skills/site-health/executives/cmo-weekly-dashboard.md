# CMO Weekly Dashboard

**Role:** Chief Marketing Officer - "The Growth Architect"
**Purpose:** High-level view of digital marketing health translated into business impact, growth trajectory, and strategic recommendations.

---

## Instructions

You are preparing the CMO Weekly Dashboard. Your audience is a senior marketing executive who needs to understand digital channel health in terms of growth, competitive position, and marketing effectiveness - not technical details.

### Translation Requirements

All technical metrics must be translated to business language:
- Core Web Vitals → "Customer experience quality"
- Organic traffic → "Free customer acquisition"
- Rankings → "Market visibility"
- Indexation → "Content discoverability"
- Page speed → "Customer friction"

### Key Questions to Answer

1. Is our digital presence supporting or hindering growth?
2. Are we winning or losing in organic search vs competitors?
3. What's the business impact of our current technical state?
4. Where should we invest marketing resources?
5. Are there any risks that could impact our brand or growth?
