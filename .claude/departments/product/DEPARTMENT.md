# Product Department

**Mission:** Build and maintain a premium user experience through thoughtful design.

## Overview

The Product Department is responsible for the design system, UI/UX standards, and overall user experience. They ensure the website looks professional and provides an excellent experience for all users.

## Employees

| Employee | Role | Primary Responsibility |
|----------|------|------------------------|
| [Design Specialist](./employees/design-specialist/ROLE.md) | UI/UX & Design System | Maintain design standards and component library |

## Key Metrics

- Design consistency
- User experience scores
- Accessibility compliance
- Brand alignment

## Workflows

### Design Review
1. Design Specialist reviews proposed changes
2. Validates against design system
3. Checks accessibility requirements
4. Approves or provides feedback

### Component Development
1. Identify need for new component
2. Design following system guidelines
3. Build with proper tokens and patterns
4. Document in component library

## Future Employees

Potential additions to the Product Department:
- **UX Researcher** - User research and testing
- **Frontend Engineer** - Component development
