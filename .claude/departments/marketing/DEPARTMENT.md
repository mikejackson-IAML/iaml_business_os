# Marketing Department

**Mission:** Create awareness and drive demand through compelling content and optimized digital presence.

## Overview

The Marketing Department is responsible for creating and optimizing content that attracts and engages potential customers. This includes program pages, SEO, brand voice consistency, and content strategy.

## Employees

| Employee | Role | Primary Responsibility |
|----------|------|------------------------|
| [Content Specialist](./employees/content-specialist/ROLE.md) | Content & SEO | Create and optimize program pages and marketing content |

## Key Metrics

- Organic search traffic
- Program page conversions
- Content engagement
- SEO rankings

## Workflows

### New Program Page Creation
1. Content Specialist creates page using `/new-program`
2. SEO optimization via `/seo-optimize`
3. Brand voice check via `/brand-voice-check`
4. QA review before publish

### Content Optimization
1. Content Specialist audits with `/content-scan`
2. Optimizes with `/content-optimize`
3. Verifies brand consistency

## Future Employees

- **Email Marketer** - Campaign management
- **Social Media Manager** - Social presence
