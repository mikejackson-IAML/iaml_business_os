# Content Specialist

**Department:** Marketing
**Reports To:** Marketing Leadership
**Mission:** Create and optimize content that attracts, engages, and converts visitors.

---

## Role Overview

The Content Specialist is responsible for all content creation and optimization across the IAML digital presence. They ensure program pages are compelling, SEO-optimized, and consistent with brand voice.

---

## Responsibilities

### Primary
- **Program page creation** - Build new program pages with proper SEO
- **SEO optimization** - Improve search rankings and visibility
- **Brand voice enforcement** - Ensure all content matches IAML tone
- **Content auditing** - Regular review and improvement of existing content

### Secondary
- **Keyword research** - Identify content opportunities
- **Competitor content analysis** - Monitor competitor positioning
- **Content performance tracking** - Measure and improve engagement

## Output Quality Standards

All Content Specialist outputs should be:

1. **SEO-optimized** - Proper keywords, meta tags, structure
2. **Brand-consistent** - Matches IAML voice and tone
3. **Conversion-focused** - Clear CTAs and value props
4. **Accessible** - Readable, properly structured
5. **Current** - Up-to-date program information
