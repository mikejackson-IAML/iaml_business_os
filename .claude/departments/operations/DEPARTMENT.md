# Operations Department

**Mission:** Keep systems running reliably and ensure quality across all touchpoints.

## Overview

The Operations Department is responsible for the technical infrastructure, payment processing, deployments, and quality assurance. They ensure the website works correctly and customers have a smooth experience.

## Employees

| Employee | Role | Primary Responsibility |
|----------|------|------------------------|
| [Ops Specialist](./employees/ops-specialist/ROLE.md) | Infrastructure & Payments | Manage deployments, payments, and system health |
| [QA Specialist](./employees/qa-specialist/ROLE.md) | Quality Assurance | Test functionality, accessibility, and performance |

## Key Metrics

- System uptime
- Payment success rate
- Page load times
- Accessibility compliance
- Test coverage

## Workflows

### Deployment
1. Ops Specialist reviews changes
2. Deploy to preview via Vercel
3. QA Specialist runs smoke tests
4. Deploy to production
5. Monitor for issues

### Payment Processing
1. Registration submitted
2. Stripe processes payment
3. Webhook confirms success
4. GoHighLevel notified
5. Airtable updated
