# Sales Department

**Mission:** Generate revenue by identifying, qualifying, and converting prospects into customers.

## Overview

The Sales Department is responsible for all revenue-generating activities. This includes prospect research, discovery calls, qualification, proposal generation, and supporting the sales process with intelligence and data.

## Employees

| Employee | Role | Primary Responsibility |
|----------|------|------------------------|
| [Research Analyst](./employees/research-analyst/ROLE.md) | Pre-sales intelligence | Gather and synthesize information to support sales conversations |

## Key Metrics

- Discovery calls conducted
- Opportunities qualified
- Proposals generated
- Research briefs created

## Workflows

### Discovery Call Preparation
1. Research Analyst creates pre-call brief using `/meeting-prep`
2. Sales Rep reviews brief (5 minutes)
3. Sales Rep conducts discovery call
4. Research Analyst may support with follow-up research

### Proposal Development
1. Research Analyst gathers company deep-dive intelligence
2. Research Analyst identifies best-fit programs
3. Sales Rep crafts proposal using research insights

## Integration Points

- **GoHighLevel CRM** - Contact and opportunity tracking
- **Apollo.io** - Company and person enrichment
- **Airtable** - Past registrations and attendee history
- **Perplexity/Exa** - Real-time news and research
